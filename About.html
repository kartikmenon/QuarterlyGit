About

The Dartmouth Quarterly of East Asian Studies is an academic research journal that publishes undergraduate and graduate work from around the world.

The Quarterly is an entirely student-run operation advised by three Dartmouth College professors and aims to publish East Asian affairs in a fashion accessible to a general audience.

The Quarterly was established in 2014 and will publish its inaugural issue in the Fall of 2014. Submissions for the fall issue are now being accepted here.
