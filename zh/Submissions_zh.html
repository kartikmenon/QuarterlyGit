Submissions

  • Submission Information (click to expand)
    • Guidelines

      • - Articles should be research related Japan, China, or Korea, or a combination.


        - Please keep your submission between 3000 and 8000 words in length.

        Exceptional submissions outside this limit will also be considered, though the author may be asked to edit their work.


        - Please supply a 100-300 word abstract together with your submission, outlining your topic and

        major points or findings.


        - The submission document should be in 12-point font with one-inch margins.

        Acceptable submission formats are: .doc, .docx, .txt, .rtf, .pages


        - Please don't include any personal information (e.g., name, university) in your submission document.

        This way your submission will be evaluated anonymously.


        - Please follow the Chicago Manual of Style for all references.


        - Please clearly explain or avoid technical terms related to East Asia.
    • How to submit

      • - Submissions should be emailed to ________@dartmouth.edu.


        Use the following format when emailing:


        Subject Line: [Submission Name] [Your Name]


        Email Body: Include the following information.

        1. Full Name

        2. Email address for future correspondence

        3. Gender

        4. Geographic Area of Research (China, Japan, Korea, or a combination)

        5. University Affiliation

        6. University Department

        7. Field(s) of Research (e.g., culture, linguistics, etc.)

        8. Year of Graduation

        9. Anticipated Degree (e.g., B.A., PhD)

        10. Title of Submission


        - Once received, a confirmation email will be sent to you.

        - Work that has been previously published in another undergraduate or graduate level journal

        will not be considered for publication.


        - The editorial board of the Quarterly reserves the right to accept or refuse a publication.
